List all of the roles (and their IDs) in the server, or all the roles that a particular user has.


# Usage

>vc/listroles
vc/listroles `USER`

# Examples

- `vc/listroles`
 lists all of the roles in the server(and their IDs)
- `vc/listroles pixaal`
Lists all of pixaal's roles(and their IDs)
