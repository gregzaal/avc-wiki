Transfer ownership of your channel to someone else in the channel, allowing them to use commands that require them to be the creator (e.g. `private`, `limit`, `name` etc.).

# Usage

vc/alias `@USER`



# Examples

* `vc/transfer @pixaal`
 Transfers ownership to the user @pixaal
